Sheeps and Rams

Rules:

  1. The computer selects a four digit number, all four digits are different.
  2. In the current implementation a number may not begin with 0.
  3. The Sheep column displays the total number of digits you guessed right but are not in the right place.
  4. The Ram column shows all the digits put into their right spots.
  5. Play by entering your guess in the box below and click "Check" to validate your entry. You may also click "New" to start another game.
